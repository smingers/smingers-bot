# Outside View (Base Rate) Estimation

## Question
Will Ali Khamenei cease to be Supreme Leader of Iran before 2027?

## Description
Ali Khamenei has been the Supreme Leader of Iran since 1989. The Supreme Leader is the highest-ranking political and religious authority in Iran and holds significant power over the government and military. Khamenei is currently 83 years old, and there is speculation about his health and potential succession.

## Resolution Criteria
For the purposes of this analysis, "cease to be Supreme Leader" means that Khamenei either dies, resigns, or is removed from power before the end of 2026.

## Your Task

The historical record on leadership transitions in authoritarian regimes can be challenging to obtain and may be subject to biases. However, there are some reliable sources that track leadership changes in countries like Iran, such as academic studies and reports from think tanks and international organizations.
