- **Pros:** Captures “escalation recurrence” after a barrier to first use is crossed (important here because the US already struck Iranian territory in June 2025).
- **Cons:** Also small N for this exact dyad; recurrence depends heavily on retaliation dynamics and negotiations.

**Most suitable outside-view anchor:** a **blend of (1) and (2)**, with an adjustment for “barrier already crossed” from (3). In plain terms: historically rare, but once you’re already in a hot crisis with deployments and explicit threats, the conditional base rate rises sharply.
