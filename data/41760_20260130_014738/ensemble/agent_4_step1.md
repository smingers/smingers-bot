Add deterministic Jan–Apr seasonal climb (+1.8 ppm) → 429.29 ppm.  
Add baseline secular rise: 2.3 ppm between Apr 2025 and Apr 2026 (consistent with recent non-El Niño years; Met Office gives 2.26 ± 0.56 ppm for 2025).  Therefore Apr 2026 central estimate = Apr 2025 + 2.3 ppm.  Apr 2025 can be reconstructed from Tech Explorist May 2025 430.2 ppm minus typical May–Apr difference (0.8 ppm) → 429.4 ppm.  429.4 + 2.3 = 431.7 ppm (cross-check with December-anchored method: 427.49 + 1.8 + 2.3 ≈ 431.6 ppm).  The two paths agree within 0.1 ppm—gives confidence.

Uncertainty budget (one-sigma):  
• Meteorological noise in monthly mean: 0.1 ppm  
• ENSO uncertainty in annual increment: 0.5 ppm  
• Measurement/processing prelim status: 0.05 ppm  
Quadrature σ ≈ sqrt(0.1²+0.5²+0.05²) ≈ 0.51 ppm – dovetails with historical April increment SD.
