(d) Justification for outside-view estimate  
Baseline: April 2025 monthly mean. From NOAA raw file (downloaded 30 Jan 2026) this is 428.98 ppm.  
Add expected anthropogenic increment: central 2.2 ppm (Met Office 2.26 ± 0.56, adjusted −0.2 ppm for La Niña influence).  
Seasonal shape year-to-year is tightly locked; April sits 2.6 ppm above the concurrent annual mean with σ ≈ 0.10 ppm. Therefore year-on-year growth is a good proxy for April → April change.  
Central projection: 428.98 + 2.2 = 431.18 ppm.

Uncertainty components (treated as independent, summed in quadrature):  
• Anthropogenic rise: σ ≈ 0.55 ppm (Met Office).  
• ENSO mis-forecast / carbon-cycle noise: σ ≈ 0.35 ppm (derived from SD of detrended April jumps).  
• Measurement/final-calibration tweak: σ ≈ 0.10 ppm.  
Combined σ ≈ 0.66 ppm.
