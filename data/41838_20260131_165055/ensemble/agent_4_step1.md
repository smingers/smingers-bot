(d) Justification for the outside-view baseline  
1. Anchor on Q4-24 EMEA.  Alphabet did not publish Q4-24 geography, but applying the 29 % mix to estimated Q4-24 total constant-currency revenue of ~92 bn USD gives ≈ 26.7 bn USD.  
2. Growth drivers to Q4-25:  
   • Company-wide YoY growth momentum: Search + YouTube ads tracking mid-teens; Cloud mid-20s.  • FX tail-wind: euro up ~4 % vs USD YoY; but question asks constant-currency, so neutralised.  
   • Macro in Europe / Mideast: mixed but advertising PMI points to mild positive growth; no major shock.  
   • Therefore assume EMEA grows slightly below consolidated pace (Europe softer than U.S.), i.e. +12 % to +18 % YoY, mode ~15 %.  
3. Multiply 26.7 bn × 1.15 ⇒ 30.7 bn modal estimate.  
4. Uncertainties: (i) actual Q4-25 total revenue could span 102–112 bn USD; (ii) EMEA mix 28–30 %; (iii) execution variability ±2 pp.  Monte-Carlo on those bands yields a 90 % central range of roughly 29–33 bn USD.  
5. Cross-check: the lone Yahoo consensus (32.4 bn) sits toward the high side but inside that range, lending face validity.
