> The S\&P CoreLogic Case-Shiller Home Price Indices are based on observed changes in home prices. In contrast, the indices are, specifically, not intended to measure recovery costs after disasters, construction or repair costs, or other such related items.The indices are calculated monthly, using a three-month moving average algorithm. Home sales pairs are accumulated in rolling three-month periods, on which the repeat sales methodology is applied.
