(d) Justification
Base rate from reference class #1: 22 % chance of a ≥5 pp upward move — enough to push the CP from exactly 25 % to >30 %.  However, the threshold here is infinitesimal: any uptick to 25.01 % counts as “higher”.  Assuming a roughly symmetric ±3 pp sigma around the 25 % starting point, a pure random-walk outside view would assign ~50 % to finishing above.  

We should tilt that 50 % downward because:
• Directional news flow since mid-January has been mildly NEGATIVE for the prospect of a purchase offer (shift from “buy” language to “leases/mineral rights”).  
• No scheduled diplomatic event falls between now and 15 Feb except an announced Rubio-Vance meeting that was to occur on 11 Feb (four days before the deadline).  Even if something surprising came out, it would likely leak first, giving investors/forecasters at most a few hours to react.  The historical record shows leaks of that magnitude move CP 5-15 pp, but such leaks are rare (≈10 % frequency).  
• The Metaculus community is already unusually attentive to Greenland news because of repeated prior questions; surprise factor is lower, implying smaller average reactions.

Putting these together:
– 50 % (pure symmetry)  
– minus 10 % for mildly negative news trend  
– minus 5 % for low remaining forecasting activity  
→ 35 % probability that CP > 25 % on 15 Feb 2026.
