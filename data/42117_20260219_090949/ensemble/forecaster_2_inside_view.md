2. **Structural disadvantage in direct mandates:** New electoral system awards 70 constituency seats. CDU expected to win 30-40, AfD "low single digits" (per agent report). This creates insurmountable seat gap even if party-list votes were equal.
3. **Historical precedent:** 0/35 western German state elections have AfD finishing first. Zero instances of third-place party (9 points behind) winning in final 18 days.

**Evidence weight:** Overwhelming evidence against AfD plurality. No credible pathway identified in any source.
