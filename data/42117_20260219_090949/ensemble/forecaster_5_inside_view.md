Moderate evidence

• FDP (~5 %) or BSW (~4 %) falling below the threshold could enlarge AfD’s seat share but would enlarge CDU’s even more.  

Weak evidence  
• Potential “shy AfD” polling error (historic mean <2  pp).  
• Frohnmaier foreign-trip optics could marginally help or hurt turnout.
