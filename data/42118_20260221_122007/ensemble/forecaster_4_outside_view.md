Even optimistically assuming a “fast-track” timeline of 120 days, the window left (70 days) is only 58% of that. Generously assign 10% chance of finishing in time.  

   0.25% × 0.10 = 0.025%.  

4. Model uncertainty / unknown unknowns: bump up slightly (factor 2) to guard against tail risks (e.g., an unopposed case decided summarily).  

   Final outside-view probability ≈ 0.05%.
