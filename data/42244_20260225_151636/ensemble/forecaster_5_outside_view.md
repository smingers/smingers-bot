Historical record: in the last 26 Masters only 6 favourites with odds shorter than 10 / 1 have won (≈23 %).  However 23 of 26 winners were inside the OWGR top 25.  Therefore I take as reference class: “OWGR-top-25 players with pre-event odds < 30 / 1”.

In each of the last 11 Masters the eventual winner was already inside the top-10 of the betting market six weeks out.  Thus the current market is a good outside-view proxy.
