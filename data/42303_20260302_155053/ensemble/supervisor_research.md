## Summary: 2022 Slovenian Parliamentary Election Results

**Key Electoral Outcomes:**

- **Freedom Movement (GS/Gibanje Svoboda)** led by Robert Golob won **41 of 90 seats** with **34.54%** of the vote — a decisive first-place finish
- **Slovenian Democratic Party (SDS)** led by Janez Janša finished second with **27 seats** and **23.53%** of the vote (+2 seats vs. 2018)
- The margin of victory was a surprise, as pre-election polls had shown the two parties neck-and-neck
- **GS won 14 more seats than SDS** in 2022 (41 vs. 27)

**Other Parties:**
- New Slovenia (NSi): 8 seats (6.85%)
- Social Democrats (SD): 7 seats (6.65%)
- The Left (L): 5 seats (4.38%, barely cleared the 4% threshold)

**Turnout:** 69.54%, the highest since 2000 (+16.91 points vs. 2018)

**Notable Analyst Opinions:**
- Miha Kovac (University of Ljubljana): described the vote as being "against Janez Janša" and against illiberal democratic tendencies
- Uros Esih (Delo): characterized the election as "a battle between liberal and illiberal forces"
- Valdo Miheljak (University of Ljubljana): noted Slovenia had become "one of the biggest troublemakers" regarding freedoms under Janša
