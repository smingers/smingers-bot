### Key Points from the Article:

**Treasury Par Yield Curve (CMT) Methodology:**
- Yields are interpolated daily using a **monotone convex spline method** based on closing market bid prices of the most recently auctioned Treasury securities.
- Prices are obtained by the Federal Reserve Bank of New York at approximately **3:30 PM each trading day**.
- CMT yields are read at fixed maturities including **10 years**, even if no outstanding security has exactly that remaining maturity.
- The floor for nominal CMT yields is **zero** (negative yields are not published in the CMT series).

**Series Notes:**
- The 1.5-month CMT series began **February 18, 2025**.
- The 4-month CMT series began **October 19, 2022**.
