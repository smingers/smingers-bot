Baseline (outside view) = status-quo value 1.93 %.  
Drivers of a deviation ≥ 5 bp in the next week:  
• Unexpected inter-meeting ECB action – historically < 0.5 % probability.  
• Severe funding-stress episode (e.g., pandemic 2020, UK LDI 2022) – probability around 1 % in any random week, would push €STR up if banks scramble for liquidity.

Combining these, odds of a ≥ 5 bp move within 6 business days are ~1.5  %. For ordinary micro-fluctuations (±1–2 bp) empirical 1-week σ ≈ 1 bp. Therefore a 90 % central band ±1.5 bp around 1.93 % is defensible, with very fat, but very low-weight, tails for extraordinary events.
