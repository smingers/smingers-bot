## Net evidentiary judgment

- **“Will fighting de-escalate?”** and
- **“Will there be an official bilateral ceasefire in force before May 1?”**

2. possible tacit or brokered de-escalation,

4. but **not yet** a mutually announced all-kinetic bilateral ceasefire.
