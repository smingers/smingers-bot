# Research Plan: Will any Google Gemini model rank #1 on the LMSYS (LM Arena) leaderboard on March 15, 2026?

PART 1: Key uncertainties (what a forecaster most needs to pin down)

1. Current standing and recent trajectory of Gemini models on LM Arena  
   Why it matters: If Gemini is already #1 (or very close and climbing) on 4 March 2026, the base-rate chance of it being #1 in ten days is much higher.

2. Imminent model releases or upgrades from Google or competitors before 15 March 2026  
   Why it matters: A just-released Gemini-family upgrade could vault to #1; likewise an upcoming OpenAI, Anthropic, or Mistral release could knock Gemini down.

3. Frequency and volatility of LM Arena leaderboard updates  
   Why it matters: Knowing how quickly new conversational votes move the scores tells us whether a leadership change in the next ten days is plausible.

4. Whether Google is actively submitting/refreshing Gemini checkpoints on LM Arena  
   Why it matters: Even a superior Gemini version won’t rank #1 if Google hasn’t uploaded it. Evidence of recent Google participation increases the chance.

5. Historical prevalence and handling of ties at #1  
   Why it matters: The question resolves YES if any tied #1 is Gemini, so the probability of a tie is directly relevant.
