## 7. Recommendations

### Research Improvements

1. **Verify legislative claims:** When research surfaces specific claims about bill status (number of co-sponsors, presidential endorsement), add a verification step to check primary sources (congress.gov, official White House statements)
2. **Resolve contradictory research outputs:** When two research runs produce opposite conclusions ("unlikely" vs "highly likely"), flag this for special handling
3. **Add legislative tracker queries:** Include queries specifically targeting bill status and committee hearing schedules

### Prompt/Pipeline Improvements

1. **Instruct agents to be skeptical of AskNews "Deep Research Analysis" conclusions:** These are synthesized opinions, not primary facts
2. **Add cross-reference requirement:** When a claim appears in only one source, require agents to note this limitation
3. **Consider outlier detection:** When one agent's forecast differs by >20pp from others, flag for review

### Model-Specific Feedback

- **o3 (Agent 4):** Showed susceptibility to accepting claims that aligned with its base rate direction without adequate verification. Consider adding explicit instruction to verify claims that significantly support the current hypothesis.
- **Sonnet 4.5 (Agents 1, 2):** Performed excellently with appropriate skepticism of unverified claims
- **GPT-5.2 (Agent 3):** Good performance with appropriate upward revision from low S1 input
