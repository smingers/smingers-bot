## 7. Recommendations

### Research Improvements
1. **Meta-question specific queries:** For questions about Metaculus community predictions, add a dedicated query to retrieve the current community prediction value from Metaculus directly.
2. **Query differentiation:** Historical and current queries had substantial overlap; design queries to be more complementary.
3. **Prediction market coverage:** Retrieve actual prices from Polymarket and other markets, not just their existence.

### Prompt/Pipeline Improvements
1. **Base rate standardization:** Consider providing a consistent reference for historical SCOTUS composition change rates to reduce cross-forecaster variation.
2. **Meta-question guidance:** Add specific guidance for meta-questions that distinguishes between forecasting the underlying event and forecasting community beliefs about the event.
3. **Spread warning:** When ensemble spread exceeds 30pp on a binary question, trigger a reconciliation step.
