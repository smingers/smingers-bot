## 7. Recommendations

### Research Improvements
- **Fix FRED series matching** -- the query "Germany Ifo Business Climate Index" should be mapped to the correct FRED series identifier. Consider using known series IDs directly for common economic indicators.
- **Improve Agent task completion** -- the Agent should be configured to either compute the requested statistics or fail fast. Two steps of meta-description about data availability is not useful.
- **Filter Investing.com results** -- this source has now returned only disclaimers in both the 42038 and 42040 forecasts. Consider blocklisting it or detecting empty/disclaimer-only content and re-querying.

### Prompt/Pipeline Improvements
- **Add tail calibration guidance to inside view prompt** -- something like "Your P10-P90 range should be consistent with the historical standard deviation of changes over the forecast horizon. For a 2-month forecast of a monthly economic indicator with sigma ~1.5 per month, the 2-month sigma is ~2.1, implying P10-P90 of approximately plus/minus 2.7 points."
- **Condition the outside view prompt more explicitly on the current level** -- the unconditional distribution error from S1-3 (and previously S1-4 of 42038) could be prevented by adding language like "Your distribution should be centered near the most recent known value unless you have strong reason to believe a regime change is occurring."
- **Detect and flag outlier CDFs before aggregation** -- if one CDF's interquartile range is >2x the median IQR of other CDFs, log a warning and consider down-weighting.

### Model-Specific Feedback
- **GPT-5.2 (Forecaster 3):** Recurring issue with unconditional distribution selection for outside view (seen in both 42038/S1-4 and 42040/S1-3). This model interprets "outside view" too literally as ignoring all conditioning information, including the current level. Needs stronger prompt guidance or a different reference class prompt structure.
- **o3 (Forecasters 4-5):** Consistently produces wider distributions than other models, especially in inside view. Both S2-4 and S2-5 generated 12-point 80% intervals when 5-6 points would be appropriate. The o3 model may benefit from explicit width constraints or calibration anchors in the prompt.
- **Sonnet 4.5 (Forecasters 1-2):** Consistently strong performance across both stages. Produces well-calibrated distributions with appropriate width and centering. Could provide slightly more diversity between the two instances.
