## 7. Recommendations

### Research Improvements
- For forward-looking weekly forecasts, add explicit research on shutdown resolution timeline (Congressional voting calendar, funding negotiations)
- Include spring break calendar data for major school districts to assess late-Feb travel demand
- Pull prior-year same-week data as a mandatory anchor

### Prompt/Pipeline Improvements
- **Lower the numeric divergence threshold**: The current threshold missed clear cases of ensemble disagreement across all three TSA questions
- **Add growth rate validation**: When forecasters cite early-period YoY growth, require comparison against the specific target week's historical YoY pattern
- **Consider trimmed mean or median aggregation**: For this question, the trimmed mean of S2-1, S2-3, S2-4 (~17.2M) would likely be better calibrated than the full equal-weighted average
- **Supervisor model should handle bimodal disagreement**: The ensemble split (growth vs. disruption camps) reflects a genuine analytical question that a supervisor could resolve with targeted research on shutdown precedent timing

### Model-Specific Feedback
- **Sonnet 4.5 (S2-2)**: Can over-correct when given a "crisis" framing. Should maintain that the median should reflect the most likely scenario, not the worst-case.
- **o3 (S2-5)**: Persistent tendency to apply recent growth rates without adjusting for target-period-specific seasonality.
- **GPT-5.2 (S2-3)**: Consistently the best performer on these TSA questions. Appropriate skepticism about growth rate persistence.
