## 7. Recommendations

### Research Improvements
- Add a specific "same-week-prior-year" lookup as a mandatory research step for recurring time-series questions
- Flag when early-period growth rates significantly differ from same-period-prior-year patterns

### Prompt/Pipeline Improvements
- **Divergence threshold needs lowering for numeric questions**: The current threshold did not catch this massive disagreement
- **Add growth rate sanity check**: When forecasters apply YoY growth, prompt should verify that the specific target period exhibited similar growth historically
- **Consider trimmed mean aggregation**: Removing the highest and lowest forecaster before averaging would have produced a much better result here (median ~17.1M from S2-1, S2-3, S2-4)

### Model-Specific Feedback
- **Sonnet 4.5 (S2-2)**: Tendency to over-weight recent momentum data and dismiss structural risks. Should be prompted to compare growth rates across specific calendar weeks, not just periods.
- **o3 (S2-5)**: Fat-tail calibration can produce unrealistic extremes (P90 of 21.6M). Should be prompted to cross-check tail percentiles against known system capacity.
- **GPT-5.2 (S2-3)**: Best performer on this question. Appropriate anchoring and conservative adjustments.
